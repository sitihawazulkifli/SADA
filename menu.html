About SADA

Syarikat Air Darul Aman (SADA) a wholly owned subsidiary of Kedah Darul Aman and is the successor company to the Kedah Water Supply Department, which was incorporated on January 1,2010. The corporatized Jabatan Bekalan Air (JBA) is due to an amendment of the federal constitution. Water Services Industry. Syarikat Air Darul Aman (SADA) operate as corporations, concerned with the administration of quality and safeguard the interests of the people of Kedah to get clean water to reach the standards of the Ministry of Health and World Health Organization (WHO). SADA also supplies clean water to the people of the state with cheaper rates as compared to other states.
